Silent Book

Brief /Description:

The "My Old House by the Hill" project was a university assignment focused on illustrating a silent book that narrates a woman’s journey through grief. The goal was to use illustrations to add depth and emotional resonance to the story, making it more impactful and engaging.

Client

University Project

Project

Illustration
— Silent Book

Role

Illustration

Solution/Outcome:

—I utilized Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to create illustrations that brought depth and emotional resonance to the silent book’s narrative. The illustrations were designed to complement and enhance the story’s themes of grief and recovery, using visual elements to convey complex emotions and key narrative moments effectively. Each illustration was carefully crafted to align with the book’s tone and message.

...

The illustrations successfully added significant value to the narrative, providing a visual interpretation that enriched the reader’s experience. The project was praised for its emotional impact and ability to visually narrate the story. Future work could explore different illustration styles and techniques to further enhance storytelling and emotional engagement.

Reflection/Learnings:

—The illustrations played a crucial role in adding emotional depth and enhancing the narrative. This project showcased the power of visual storytelling and underscored the importance of aligning illustrations with the narrative’s emotional tone. Future projects could benefit from exploring various illustration styles and techniques to further enrich the storytelling experience.
