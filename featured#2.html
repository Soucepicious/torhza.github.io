Liyam's Prototype

Brief /Description:

—"The Liyam’s Prototype project involved designing a personal website/portfolio as a university project. The aim was to create an interactive and user-friendly web experience that would effectively showcase my work, interests, and professional background.

Client

University Project

Project

Web Design
— Portfolio

Role

UX/UI Design

Solution/Outcome:

—I used Adobe Photoshop and XD to develop a personal website that showcased my portfolio, beliefs, and background. The design focused on creating a smooth user experience with intuitive navigation and interactive features like animations and hover effects. Wireframes and prototypes were meticulously crafted to ensure that the final website was both visually appealing and functional, representing my personal brand effectively.

...

The outcome was a polished and functional website that showcased my portfolio while providing a comprehensive view of my professional background and interests. Adobe XD played a crucial role in creating a smooth and responsive prototype, allowing for an effective demonstration of the design's usability and interaction.

Reflection/Learnings:

—The website was well-received for its engaging and intuitive user experience, which enhanced my personal branding and portfolio visibility. Future improvements could include exploring additional interactive features and refining the website’s responsive design to further enhance user experience.
